Our profile

Expertise and professional understanding

At JUNOD TAX, you will find people who know the answers to your questions. You may therefore place your trust in them with full confidence and look forward to a lasting and fruitful professional relationship. In working on your behalf they enjoy the full support and resources of our highly trained back-office team.

Fields of activity

Our clients are local and international companies in all areas of business. We also advise private individuals, associations and incorporated bodies. We primarily focus on providing tax and legal advice to companies and entrepreneurs, while never losing sight of the broader picture as it affects our clients.

Added value

JUNOD TAX offers its expertise to your advantage, consulting with its local, national and international networks as and when required. We seek out global, lasting and innovative solutions, while steering clear of instant, short-lived successes. Client benefit is our priority and we therefore make a point of defining relevant issues in personal consultation with clients so as to be able to provide tailor-made services. Our code of professional secrecy binds us to total discretion.

Independence

We are very proud of our independence. JUNOD TAX avoids conflicts of interest and works in strict conformity with professional standards. Teamwork that also brings in outside specialists as and when required - subject to our client's approval - enables us to apply all the requisite know-how needed for successful results. We do all we can to be flexible and to be there for our clients whenever they need us.

Fees

Our fees are computed on a time-spent basis and according to the experience of the staff involved. We are committed to transparency and efficiency in matters of cost.

Training

We regard continuing professional education as a major factor in our ability to provide top class services.
